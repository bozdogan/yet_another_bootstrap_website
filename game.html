    Step 1

  • five bricks (red: 20p, blue: 40p, yellow: 50p, purple: 60p and green: 80p), a black obstacle, light blue paddle, light blue ball.
  • Location of the bricks must be as specified below figure.
  • There must be “Score” text out of canvas.

    • Step 2

  • Ball moves up and down, paddle moves right and left.

    • Step 3

  • Ball starts to move as the games begins.
  • If the ball is touching the bottom wall of canvas, then alert is shown and game will be over.

    • Step 4

  • If the ball is touching any brick, then the brick must be disappered and score must be increased.

    • Step 5

  • Ball performs elastic collision on walls and the obstacle

  • and then it changed the direction of movement down.

  • When the brick is broken, the ball color must be color of broken brick.
